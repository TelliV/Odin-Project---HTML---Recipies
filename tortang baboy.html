Lechon Kawali

Description

Lechon Kawali is basically fried pork belly.

Ingredients

  • 0.5 kg Pork Belly
  • 4 cups Water
  • 3 thumbs Ginger
  • 1 Red Onion
  • 3 tbsp Patis
  • 10 cloves Garlic
  • 1 tbsp Black peppercorn
  • 5 tbsp Salt
  • 4 cups Vegetable Oil

Directions

  1. In a pot, add ginger, onion, garlic and black peppercorn, patis, and salt and let it boil for 10 minutes
  2. Add the pork belly and let it cook for 15 minutes.
  3. Remove the pork belly and let it cool to room temperature.
  4. Heat up the vegetable oil to 175 °C and rfy pork belly for 20 minutes.
  5. Remove pork belly and let it cool for 10 minutes.
  6. Fry pork belly again in 195 °C.
  7. Remove pork belly, cut it in to small pieces, and serve!
