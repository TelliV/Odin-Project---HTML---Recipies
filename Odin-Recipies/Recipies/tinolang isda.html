Tinolang Isda

Description

Tinolang Isda is a Filipino light soup made up of vegetable stock, vegetables, and fish.

Ingredients

  • 0.5 kg Mackerel
  • 2 cups Alugbati
  • 3 thumbs Ginger
  • 2 Tomato wedges
  • 1 stalk Lemongrass
  • 1 Red Onion
  • 3 tbsp Patis

Directions

  1. Pour water in a soup pot. Let boil
  2. Add the ginger, tomato, lemongrass, and red onion. Let it boil for 5-8 minutes
  3. Add the mackerel and let it boil for 12-15 minutes
  4. Add the alugbati and patis and let it boil for 2 mintues
  5. Transfer soup to a serving bowl and enjoy!
